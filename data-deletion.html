Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: October 2025

This Data Deletion Policy explains how DemandMind handles deletion of Shopify store data when you uninstall the app or request removal.

Automatic Data Deletion

When a merchant uninstalls DemandMind:

  • Shopify sends an automatic “app uninstall” webhook.
  • DemandMind immediately flags all related store data for removal.
  • All store data is fully deleted from DemandMind systems within 48 hours.

This includes:

  • Order history.
  • SKU/product metadata.
  • Forecast output.
  • Logs associated with your store.
  • Any cached or temporary forecasting data.

We do not retain any identifiable data after deletion.

Data We Do NOT Store

To protect your privacy, DemandMind does not store:

  • Customer names, emails, or other personal data.
  • Payment information.
  • Any Shopify admin information unrelated to forecasting.

DemandMind only stores what is required to run forecasting models.

For any questions about data deletion, contact: demand.mind.shopify@gmail.com
