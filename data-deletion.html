Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: October 2025

This Data Deletion Policy explains how DemandMind handles deletion of Shopify store data when you uninstall or request removal. Automatic Data Deletion When a merchant uninstalls DemandMind: Shopify sends an automatic “app uninstall” webhook DemandMind immediately flags all related store data for removal All store data is fully deleted from DemandMind systems within 48 hours This includes: Order history SKU/product metadata Forecast output Logs associated with your store Any cached or temporary forecasting data We do not retain any identifiable data after deletion. Data We Do NOT Store To protect your privacy: We do not store customer names, emails, or personal data We do not store payment information We do not store any Shopify admin information unrelated to forecasting DemandMind only stores what is required to run forecasting models.

For any questions about data deletion, contact: demand.mind.shopify@gmail.com
