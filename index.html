DemandMind

Daily demand forecasts for your online store.

Connect your online store, and get clear 7–60 day sales forecasts by SKU, plus simple charts and exportable reports.

The problem

Most ecommerce merchants guess future sales based on “feel”, spreadsheets, or last-minute reorders. That leads to stockouts, overstock, and missed revenue.

The solution

DemandMind your order history to forecast what you’ll sell in the next few weeks.

  • Daily forecasts for the next 7, 30, and 60 days
  • Top SKUs and simple bar charts
  • Exportable Excel and PDF reports
  • Accuracy tracking so you can trust the numbers

FAQ

Does this change my theme or storefront?

No. The app only reads your orders data to generate forecasts. It does not modify your theme.

How often is data refreshed?

Forecasts are updated daily based on your latest orders.

Do I need to know statistics?

No. The app gives you simple tables, charts, and exports you can use right away.
