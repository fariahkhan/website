FAQ

Does DemandMind change my theme or storefront?

No. DemandMind only reads your orders data to generate forecasts. It does not modify your theme or storefront.

How often are forecasts updated?

Forecasts are refreshed whenever you sync data, so you can track how demand shifts weekly or monthly as your store evolves.

Do I need to configure models or tune anything?

No. DemandMind handles the AI and modeling behind the scenes. You just connect your store and read the tables and charts.

Can I export the forecasts?

Yes. Depending on your plan, you can export CSV, Excel (XLSX), and PDF snapshots to share with your team or plug into your existing reporting workflows.
